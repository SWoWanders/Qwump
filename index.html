QWUMP!

HIGH SCORE: 000000

HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Claim 75% of the playfield to advance to the next level.

✏️ HOW TO DRAW

1. You start on the GREEN BORDER

2. Press RIGHT to move INTO the black area

3. This starts DRAWING a line (cyan)

4. Move around, then return to any GREEN BORDER

5. The area fills in!

Tip: Start with small boxes!

🎮 CONTROLS

D-PAD: Move (start from border!)

FAST: Hold to move 2x speed

⚠️ ENEMIES

QWUMP - Purple spark in center. Don't let it touch your line!

SPARX - Red sparks on borders. Avoid!

FUSE - Orange spark that follows your line if you're too slow!

💡 STRATEGY

• Draw small areas first (10-20%)

• Don't draw long lines - the Fuse will get you!

• Use FAST mode only for short distances

• Trap Qwump in a tiny corner
