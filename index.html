HOW TO PLAY

🎯 OBJECTIVE

Claim 75% of the playfield to advance.

✏️ HOW TO DRAW

1. You start on the GREEN BORDER (left side)

2. Press RIGHT arrow to move INTO the black area

3. This starts DRAWING a line!

4. Move around, then return to any GREEN BORDER

5. The area fills in!

🎮 CONTROLS

D-PAD: Move (start from border!)

FAST: Hold to move faster

⚠️ ENEMIES

QWUMP - Purple spark. Don't touch your line!

SPARX - Red sparks on borders. Avoid!

FUSE - Follows your line if you're slow!
